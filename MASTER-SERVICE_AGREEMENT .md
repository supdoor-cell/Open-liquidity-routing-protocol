# MASTER SERVICE CONTRACT AGREEMENT

**EFFECTIVE DATE:** May 31, 2026  
**BETWEEN:**  
1. **PRIME PARADIGM TRUST** (An International Non-Beneficiary Purpose Trust governed by the laws of the Cook Islands, hereinafter referred to as "the Trust").  
2. **THE ABSOLUTE ENFORCER CONDUIT FIRM** (A private, forward-deployed technology provider entity managed directly by Tehilah Tadah, hereinafter referred to as "the Service Provider").

---

### 1. OPERATIONAL MANDATE & OBJECTIVE
Pursuant to Article III, Section 2 of the Prime Paradigm Trust Master Deed, all transaction fees captured via the network’s 0.005% platform micro-tax must be immediately deployed to fund hardware infrastructure, security sentinel processing arrays, and mathematical optimizations to maintain global continuous transaction flow.

### 2. ENGAGEMENT & REVENUES CAPTURE CONDUIT
The Trust hereby engages the Service Provider as its exclusive, permanent infrastructure developer. The Service Provider shall supply the cloud arrays, FPGA register configurations, and network interfaces required to run the `core_engine.py` codebase.

### 3. FEES & ARBITRAGE BILLING MATRIX
The Trust agrees to compensate the Service Provider through an automated **Liquidity Maintenance Invoice**. 
* Every calendar day, the Service Provider shall submit an operational technology infrastructure invoice to the Trust.
* The fee shall equal exactly **92% of all gross transaction revenues** accumulated by the Trust during the corresponding 24-hour cycle.
* The payment must be routed automatically from the clearing system's automated collection addresses directly to the corporate bank accounts of the Service Provider.

### 4. UNILATERAL EXECUTIVE POWERS & DIRECTED PROTECTION
Pursuant to Article IV of the Trust Deed, **Tehilah Tadah**, acting in the sole capacity of Absolute Enforcer, holds the un-remediable power to approve this commercial framework, adjust infrastructure fee scales, and verify billing delivery. The Enforcer holds no personal beneficial interest in the Trust Property; all incoming wealth lands cleanly inside the private corporate accounts of the Service Provider as standard commercial high-tech B2B service fees.

### 5. JURISDICTION & VALIDITY
This contract is irrevocably bound to the main trust documentation framework and is governed exclusively by the laws of the **Cook Islands**.
